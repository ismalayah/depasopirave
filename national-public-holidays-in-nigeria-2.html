National &amp; Public Holidays in Nigeria

July 2024 · 2 minute read

This post provides information about the holiday dates in Nigeria using the 2017 calender.

Most of these national public holidays in Nigeria are subject to change especially the Islamic public holiday dates which are determined by moon signs.

If you are a civil servant and are looking to plan your year ahead, especially as a federal government worker, these updated national public holiday dates for 2017 calendar might help as you won’t be caught unawares especially when the federal government of Nigeria declares public holiday at certain dates.
