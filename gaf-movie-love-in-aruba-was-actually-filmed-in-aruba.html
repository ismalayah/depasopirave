GAF Movie Love In Aruba Was Actually Filmed In Aruba

June 2024 · 5 minute read

Love In Aruba was filmed In Aruba. Love In Aruba filming locations include Bali Retreat, Aruba Ostrich Farm, and Hyatt Place Aruba Airport Hotel.

The heartwarming story is about Amber, who joins the single father Connor in Aruba to tutor and watch over his daughter.

The film revolves around characters who come together in an unusual arrangement. The scenes in the movie are full of sunshine and warm beaches and have a happy family ending.

As devoted as Connor is to his daughter, his work is demanding and preoccupying, and Amber is beginning to see the missing pieces in their lives.

As they spend more time together, Connor comes out of his protectiveness and sees Amber in a different light.

He confesses to Amber that he feels inadequate in raising Macey, they share an intimate kiss that Connor verbally regrets and leaves Amber shaken.

Where Was Love In Aruba Filmed?

The movie Love in Aruba was filmed at Bali Retreat and Philips’s Animal Garden In Aruba.  The movie is a love story of a single dad and a teacher.

The film shared the location of Bali Retreat and Hyatt Place Aruba Airport Hotel as the main location.

Bali Retreat Aruba

Bali Retreat is a unique vacation rental property on the Caribbean island. Most of the scenes in the movie have been from the property.

The place has been inspired by tropical villas of southeast Asia. The villas are a blend of cozy interiors and a large tropical garden that surrounds them.

It has an enormous landscape with a natural cave and two swimming pools, and waterfalls, which are all seen in the movie.

Philip’s Animal Garden

The scene where they talk and go to see exotic animals are shot in Philip’s animal garden. It is a nonprofit organization that has a headquarter in Aruba.

They provide a sustainable living environment for all animals and are one of the most well-designed animal shelters in the world.

The garden owner is a visionary and contributes to the local community.

The place is developed to adopt a strong and sustainable system. It creates an outlet that can guarantee and safeguard a high-quality standard of living for the population of exotic animals at the verge of extinction in Aruba.

Hyatt Place Aruba Airport Hotel

Hyatt Place in Aruba is a beautiful hotel near Airport and is Aruba’s best attraction, from beaches to the famous Arikok National Park.

The scene where Amber comes to the Caribean Island and goes to the hotel is shot in this place.

Jolly Pirates

Jolly Pirates is an unforgettable snorkeling and cruise experience. Some of the scenes, when they were going on a trip to Aruba, were shot there.

The place is where people can spend many hours on water on the Aruba pirate cruise. It also has three sites for swimming and snorkeling with tropical fish.

GAF Movie Love In Aruba Full Cast List

Love in Aruba cast consists of Sashleigha Hightower and David McConnell in the lead role.

Sashleigha Hightower As Amber

Sashleigha Hightower is a multi-talented American actress known for her role as Amber in the movie Love in Aruba.

She plays the role of a teacher who wants to teach students in the summer holidays too. To her surprise, her sister Candice sugges a tutoring job for a 10-year-old kid.

Hightower has dedicated her entire life to appearing on various platforms and repeating her name.

David McConnell As Connor

David is one of the versatile actors which every director wants to work with. He was born on October 5, 1970 in Idaho.

He is known for movies such as The Fighting Preacher and The Ides of March.

McConnell’s character in the movie is a loving father to his daughter. As he likes to spend time with his daughter, his work is never-ending.

The movie revolves around him and his daughter finding the right balance after Amber comes into their life.

Scarlett Hazen As Macey

Scarlett is a child actor known for her work in Scarlett and My One True Love. Her career is just taking off and is sure to do wonders in the entertainment industry.

Her character in the movie Macey is very close to his dad, but they can’t spend time together because of his busy schedule.

She confesses to Amber about not having a mother and is sad because her life is void. Amber then fills the gap between Connor’s and Macey’s life.

Shae Robins As Candice

Shae portrays the character of Amber’s sister in the movie Love In Aruba.

She was the one who gave her the idea of tutoring Macey..

Robins is a well-known actor who has done movies like Identical Love and My One True Love.
