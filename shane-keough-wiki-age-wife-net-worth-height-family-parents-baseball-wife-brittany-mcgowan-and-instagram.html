Shane Keough Biography – Shane Keough Wiki

Shane Keough born Shane Paul Keough Shane Keough is an American actor and former baseball player. Shane signed with the Oakland Athletics in 2006. He played four seasons in the minor-league system with the A’s from 2007-10 as an outfielder.

During the 2010 season, Shane was diagnosed with a torn patellar tendon; an injury that would require surgery. During his rehabilitation Shane created his first company, Doc Discover; a web-platform for patients to locate the best medical professionals for their condition.

In 2013, he signed with LA Model Management. As an actor, he had roles in Spare Change (2015), Naked Dragon (2014) and Capital Games (2013). He also appeared on The Real Housewives of Orange County with his mother, Jeana Keough.

In 2016, he founded Solar Waste Reprocessing, LLC. The aim of the company was to maximize the potential energy of municipal waste. Its focus soon moved to agricultural waste; specifically recovering vital resources currently uncultivated, including soil-nutrients and water.

Shane is the founder and CEO of Global Ecosystem Solutions, Inc., a California benefit corporation focused on providing solutions for corporate, community, and environmental issues around the world.
