Sarah Paulson started her acting career right after completing her High School studies. Sarah first appeared in Talking Pictures, a play by Horton Foote, which she played at the Signature Theatre.

Sarah Paulson made her debut in the feature film, Levitation, an independent thriller movie where Paulson played the character of a woman, who discovers her pregnancy via an anonymous sexual encounter. Sarah continued to be cast in several movies back to back, including Killer Joe, The Other Sister, Held Up, What Women Want.
